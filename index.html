Research Notes

  • An Approximate Solution for Consistency Equations,

    In this note, I proposed a technique to approximate a fixed point of consistency mappings deriving from mean field inference. I converted these equations to linear systems by using a linear approximation to the sigmoid function, then obtained a closed-form solution. This idea may also be used to solve an approximate solution for other energy functionals, like the Bethe free energy.

    [Research Note]

  • A Detailed Derivation of Dirichlet Process Mixtures,

    The Dirichlet process mixtures (aka Infinite GMM) is a powerful tool for Bayesian non-parametrics. There are tremendous documents in the Internet about it, but few of them contain the detailed derivation. I wrote this note for future reference.

    [Research Note]
